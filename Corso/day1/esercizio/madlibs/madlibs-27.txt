ALICE'S UPSIDE-DOWN WORLD

Lewis Carroll's classic, "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland", as well
as its CHUBBY sequel, "Through the Looking ZEUS",
have enchanted both the young and old BANANAs for the last
27 years, Alice's STICKY adventures begin
when she RUNs down a/an THUNDERING hole and lands
in a strange and topsy-turvy SANDWICH. There she discovers she
can become a tall LEGOLAS or a small ARMY simply by
nibbling on alternate sides of a magic ARAGORN. In her travels
through Wonderland, Alice BAKEDs such remarkable
characters as the White JIGSAW, the ABUNDANT Hatter,
the Cheshire JELLYFISH, and even the Queen of BILBOs.
Unfortunately, Alice's adventures come to a/an BITTER
end when Alice awakens from her BILBO.
