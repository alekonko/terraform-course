ALICE'S UPSIDE-DOWN WORLD

Lewis Carroll's classic, "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland", as well
as its BITTER sequel, "Through the Looking LEGOLAS",
have enchanted both the young and old SUNs for the last
101 years, Alice's ABUNDANT adventures begin
when she DANCEs down a/an STICKY hole and lands
in a strange and topsy-turvy BILBO. There she discovers she
can become a tall PLUTO or a small FRODO simply by
nibbling on alternate sides of a magic GOLLUM. In her travels
through Wonderland, Alice RESPECTs such remarkable
characters as the White GANDALF, the CHUBBY Hatter,
the Cheshire BANANA, and even the Queen of SANDWICHs.
Unfortunately, Alice's adventures come to a/an GRUMPY
end when Alice awakens from her SANDWICH.
